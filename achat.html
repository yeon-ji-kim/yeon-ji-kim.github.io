Overview

A-Chat is a project management chatbot application for enterprise that helps teams to manage tasks with a chatbot. A-Bird, the chatbot of this service, systematically manages the status of team members participating in the project, team schedules, and personal schedules. It also reduces the inefficiency of redundant queries that happen during collaboration, increasing the work efficiency of team members.

Discover

Defining Problems

üè∑Ô∏è Absence of a Team's Schedule Management Program
While the number of applications for teams are increasing, there are little programs for teams to keep track of their progress and the entire schedule of the project. Existing applications have limitations in that they focus on personal schedule management, which shows the tasks only given to them.

üè∑Ô∏è Difficulties in Navigating Information
Existing project management tools are difficult to crawl large amounts of information. To increase work efficiencies, intuitive search methods are needed.

üè∑Ô∏è Increase in the Burden of Communication
It is important that individuals are not overwhelmed by communications between team members. The process of asking and checking all team members one by one in order to know the progress will reduce the work efficiency.

Our Approach

To solve defined problems, we decided to use chatbot in our service.
With the chatbot, we wanted to let the chatbot do the tiresome process that we are doing right now! With chatbot, users would be able to retrieve information with no screen changes, no scrolling.
When there is no need to ask repetitive questions about team members' work process, users will save their time and energy, therefore increasing efficiency in their works.
This will also relieve communication burden between team members.

Things to Consider When Implementing Design?

As I implemented the design with developers, I learned how to utilize design systems, how to organize design specs better when delivering them to engineers. to implement efficiently. Also, we considered flexibility to meet devices with different screen size.

What's Essential for MVP?

Since we had time limitation for implementing the tool, we had difficulties in building interpretable deep learning systems, which were complicated and time-consuming. As an alternative, we took confidence tables as our explainable features. Even though the outcome turned different from what we have planned, I learned that making time-efficient choices and compromising is necessary for teams to move on.
