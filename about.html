About

Hello! I'm Yeonji Kim, a designer currently living in Ann Arbor, MI.

One of the reasons that I am fascinated with user experience is that there is no correct answer. Depending on the group of users, norms, or technology, there can be diverse answers to a single problem. With logical evidences and different design approaches, I strive to provide best answers to design problems.

With my background in computer science, I wish to design digital solutions that deliver delightful user experiences to various groups of users. These days, I am studying XR to apply my design skills to VR/AR applications.

Feel free to reach out to me if you have any questions or just want to chat!
