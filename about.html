About

Hello! I'm Yeonji Kim, a designer / engineer, currently living in Ann Arbor, MI.
I'm a graduate student at the University of Michigan - School of Information, studying HCI, UX design and research.

Before coming to Michigan, I worked as a product manager and a UX designer in an IT startup and a UX design agency. I designed video conferencing solutions, participated in branding and designing a new website for CJ ENM, and analyzed user data of a global messenger app, LINE, with over 150 million users, proposing new ideas. I also actively engaged on personal projects as a digital product designer.

As a self-motivated designer, I wish to pursue cutting-edge design techniques and provide delightful user experiences to various groups of users. I'm always looking for opportunities to expand my horizons as a designer.

Feel free to reach out to me if you have any questions or just want to chat!
