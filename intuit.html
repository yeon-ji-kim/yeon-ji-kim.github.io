Discover

Getting the Context: Stakeholder Interview

Before diving into the project, I met different stakeholders to ask about cost management to understand the context and see what‚Äôs the real problem.

  • Data Scientist who built&designed the dashboard
  • PM for Cost on AI+Data organization
  • Business Analyst managing Cloud dashboards

As I talked to them, I learned that the value of cost management is different depending on the user. Among team leaders and individual contributors, contributors were not conscious about their projects' cost while leaders were getting notified regularly on their spendings to keep under budget. And with this notifying process, it was somewhat mannual, and the budget owner marks the cost on spreadsheet and alert each leader on slack.

Takeaways

  • The PM for Cost reaches out to teams to understand why the cost is high or ask them to optimize their cost.
  • Some usability issues: ‚ÄúIt‚Äôs not always consistent. It‚Äôs not always taking you to the same process.‚Äù
  • Need for creating insights automatically: ‚Äúsomething I should look into‚Äù, ‚Äúwaste or savings opportunities (in other dashboard)‚Äù
  • The needs of contributors and leaders would be different: At this stage, the user group that needs cost information now is leaders.

After getting an overview of the project and the problem, I narrowed down the scope to prioritize AI leaders as the main user for this project as they are the most relevant user group managing cost.
