Overview

Compared to our life before COVID-19, students and teachers are experiencing more unbearable stress and emotional and psychological difficulties such as depression and anxiety. The goal of this project was to create a web application for students and teachers under school life with COVID-19 to connect with others and relieve their depression.

Challenges

After COVID-19, our life has changed in many ways. Having time with our friends became difficult, especially to students.
According to a survey of 10,499 teenage students and teachers in Daegu, Korea, due to COVID-19, they are experiencing more emotional and psychological difficulties, such as stress experiences and depression and anxiety. For the reasons of their depression, students mentioned ‚Äúnot being able to have time with friends at school‚Äù. Teachers were also having difficulties in teaching students online since they were not able to manage all students, but just delivering one-sided teaching.
How might we help students and teachers to overcome difficulties due to COVID-19, such as lack of communication not only between cohorts, but also between students and teachers?
