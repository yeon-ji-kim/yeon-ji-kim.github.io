Overview

Compared to our life before COVID-19, students and teachers experience more unbearable stress and emotional and psychological difficulties such as depression and anxiety. The goal of this project was to create a web application for students and teachers under school life with COVID-19 to connect with others and relieve their depression.

Challenges

During COVID-19, having time with our friends has become difficult, especially for students. According to a survey of 10,499 teenage students and teachers in Daegu, Korea, they have been experiencing more emotional and psychological difficulties, such as stress experiences and depression and anxiety, due to COVID-19. For their depression, students mentioned: ‚Äúnot being able to have time with friends at school.‚Äù Teachers were also having difficulties in teaching students online since they could not manage all students but just delivering one-sided teaching.

"How might we help students and teachers to overcome difficulties due to COVID-19, such as lack of communication not only between cohorts but also between students and teachers?"

Implement

System Architecture

We implemented our website by hard-coding and using some libraries and APIs.

For visualizing analyses from mood trackers, we used wordcloud and Chart.js library. We also added the Face API from Microsoft Cognitive Service, which classifies faces in images into eight emotions.

To classify Korean sentences, we used KoNLPy, which analyzes Korean words, and scikit-learn, for building machine learning models.

Implement

Implementing a Website

To create a website based on our design works, we used Python, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript on Django framework, using Visual Studio Code.

Implement

Implementing a Machine Learning Model

I collected 36,338 sentences that were classified as negative (0) or positive (1). 70% of the sentences were used for training, and the rest were used for testing.

To classify new sentences into two groups, I used the logistic regression algorithm. As a result, the accuracy of the model was 89.17%.

Reflections

Design & Communication

By determining the proper identity of the service, I learned how to combine visual assets such as colors and fonts, considering the age and situation of the target user. I also practiced delivering my design in a form that would be easy for engineers to implement, such as designing for a responsive web.

Utilizing diverse sources of technology

Aside from school projects, this was my first time implementing a machine learning model tailored to my needs! Although I struggled from getting enough dataset for training to bringing the final model to our website, I learned methods to architect a machine learning model and finally succeeded in building it by trial-and-error. I also used an API, Face API from Microsoft, for the first time. After utilizing it, I grasped the advantages of API; efficient and easy integration. I saved my time for implementing recognization features, received high accuracy results, and easily personalized it to our platform.

Product Management
